# LyftLogic Demo (2–3 minutes)

Goal: show deterministic planning, versioning, and explicit diffs without “tracker” vibes.

## 1) Home → Training
1. Start on `/`.
1. Click `Generate Training`.
1. Call out the loading UX and that the plan is deterministic.
1. Scroll the plan and open the version snapshot / diff panel.
1. Click `Regenerate`, then open the diff view.
1. Use `Restore` to revert to the previous version.

## 2) Nutrition → Macro Calculator → Generate
1. Go to `/nutrition`.
1. Open the macro calculator.
1. Enter example inputs and apply targets.
1. Click `Generate`.
1. Point to “aims vs plan totals” (guidance, not tracking).
1. Toggle the debug panel to show deterministic validation/repair data.
1. Click `Regenerate`, then show the diff panel and call out explicit changes.
